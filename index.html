My name is Hannah Whitlock. The art I create is the way I can communicate with the world. With the help of different media and techniques I can express a variety of emotion and views without using a single word. Here you can view my research and endeavors in digital media.



Project: Glitch & Grit

The de-evolution of digital media can be seen through what society has deemed "poor" images. The projects linked below explore where "poor" images belong in the creative sphere and how perspectives on these works differ depending on varying factors, such as age or ethics. The perception of digital media is unique to every individual and with that the question of worth comes into play. The worth of digital media depends on what people allow themselves to see and feel, exposing glitch and grit to the criticism of the art world.
