Is it worth it?

Plastic surgery is the repairing, reconstructing, or reshaping of body tissue. The use of it to achieve cosmetic aesthetics has been criticized, due to the effect on body image stigmas and potential failure. Many people view the results as a destruction of natural beauty, while others believe it can enhance it. The same could be said for the use of content-adjusting features on digital media, like distortion.

You tell me.

Do filters, distortion, warping, and compression destroy an image or simply enhance it? Does it create something else entirely? Is that the goal? Was it even on purpose? Does its intention or lack thereof make it worth anything? Could it be perceived as something better or even just as worthy as its natural form ?
